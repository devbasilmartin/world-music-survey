# A Survey of American Music — Indigenous Traditions (living tradition segment)

Indigenous music is NOT a 'pre-contact era' you pass through and leave behind — it is a living, continuous tradition, and pre-contact song was never recorded or notated. So this segment works differently from the rest of the survey: rather than a false 'period recording,' these are documented field recordings and living-tradition performances, most curated with the National Museum of the American Indian on Smithsonian Folkways (all streamable). They span ceremonial, social, courting, and powwow song across many distinct nations — treat each nation's music as its own tradition, not one 'Native American' genre. A content note: some Folkways album and track titles use dated, outsider-imposed terminology. In the import file, collection tracks are listed as bare titles (they live inside compilation albums); search the album name in YouTube Music to find them.
